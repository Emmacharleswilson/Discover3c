About Us

After years of chemical treatments, heat treatments and extensions, my hair has been extensively damaged. This had forced me to really look into the health of hair and specially 3c hair. As someone who has struggled with my natural hair and seen that there is not that much information on 3c hair, I thought it was important to share what I have found out and what has helped me.
